Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Post Trade Labs (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Post Trade Labs mobile and web application and website (the “Service”).

Short version:

Trade journals stay on your device unless you sign in to sync. AI coaching sends trade context (and optional screenshots you choose) to our servers to generate feedback. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Who we are

Post Trade Labs is a chart-replay and process-coaching tool for traders. Contact: support@posttradelabs.com · www.posttradelabs.com

2. Information we collect

  • Account information — If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account identifier, email address, and display name/photo as provided by Google.
  • Trade journal data — Tickers, prices, times, shares, notes, playbook text, rules, chart preferences, and related session data you enter or import.
  • Media you choose to import — Screenshots or documents you select for trade import. Images are processed to extract trade fields and are not stored as a permanent photo library on our servers.
  • AI coaching inputs — Prompts that include trade context, candle summaries, and optional import content sent to generate coach freezes and debriefs.
  • Purchase information — Subscriptions are processed by Apple / Google and RevenueCat. We receive entitlement status (plan and credits), not your full payment card details.
  • Device / technical data — Basic app diagnostics and preferences stored on device (for example chart settings and timezone).

3. How we use information

  • Provide replay, journaling, cloud sync, and coaching features
  • Authenticate your account and sync data across devices
  • Process AI coaching and import requests you initiate
  • Manage subscriptions and credit entitlements
  • Improve reliability and respond to support requests
  • Comply with law and protect the Service

4. Where data is stored

  • On device — By default, sessions and preferences are stored locally on your phone or browser.
  • Cloud sync — If you sign in, a snapshot of your journal/settings may be stored on our cloud infrastructure (Cloudflare) tied to your account.
  • AI processing — Coaching/import requests are processed via our API and third-party AI providers (currently Google Gemini) as needed to generate responses.
  • Market data — Charts may load public market bars from third-party market-data sources.

5. Sharing

We share information only as needed to run the Service:

  • Google (sign-in)
  • Cloudflare (hosting / sync)
  • Google Gemini (AI generation you request)
  • Apple, Google Play, and RevenueCat (subscriptions)
  • Market-data providers used for charts
  • Authorities when legally required

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Permissions (iOS / Android)

  • Photo library — Optional, to import trade screenshots you select.
  • Camera — Optional, if you capture a trade screenshot in-app.

We do not use the Advertising Identifier for tracking ads.

7. Retention

Local data remains until you delete it or uninstall the app. Cloud sync data remains until you delete your account or ask us to erase it. AI request content is processed to fulfill your request and is not used by us to build a public profile of your trading.

8. Your choices

  • Use the app as a guest without signing in
  • Sign out at any time
  • Delete your account in Profile (removes cloud sync data associated with your sign-in and clears local journal data on that device)
  • Email support@posttradelabs.com for access or deletion help
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID / Google Play settings

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International users

The Service may be hosted in the United States or other regions. By using the Service you understand your information may be processed in countries with different data-protection laws than your own.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. We will post the new version with an updated date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated Policy.

12. Contact

Questions about privacy: support@posttradelabs.com
