Support

We’re here to help with the app, billing, and your account.

Email support@posttradelabs.com — we typically reply within 1–2 business days.

Common topics

  • Subscriptions — Manage or cancel in your Apple ID subscriptions (iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions) or Google Play subscriptions. Use Restore purchases in the app if entitlements look wrong after reinstall.
  • Delete account / data — Open Profile in the app → Delete account. Or email us from the address on your account.
  • Charts / market data — Some windows have sparse bars (weekends, holidays, older dates). Try editing the trade times or ticker.
  • AI coach — Needs credits on paid/free allotments and a working network connection. Feedback is educational, not trade advice.
